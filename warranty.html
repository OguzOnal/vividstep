Warranty & Return Conditions

At VividStep, we prioritize your satisfaction and aim to provide you with a seamless shopping experience. Please take a moment to review our Warranty and Return Conditions outlined below:

Warranty:

  • All footwear products are covered by a manufacturer's warranty against defects in materials and craftsmanship. The warranty period may vary depending on the brand and product.
  • To claim warranty coverage, keep your purchase receipt or order number as proof of purchase.
  • The warranty is valid for normal wear and tear, and does not cover damages caused by misuse, accidents, neglect, or unauthorized alterations.

Return Policy:

  • We accept returns within 30 days of the original purchase date.
  • To be eligible for a return, the footwear must be unused, in its original packaging, and in the same condition as received.
  • Returns are subject to a thorough inspection before approval.
  • For hygienic reasons, socks and insoles are non-returnable if their packaging has been opened.
  • Customers are responsible for the return shipping costs unless the return is due to a manufacturing defect or an error on our part.

Return Process:

  • Initiate the return process through your account on our website or by contacting our customer support.
  • After receiving return approval, securely package the item along with all original accessories.
  • Ship the package back to us using a trackable shipping method.
  • Once we receive and inspect the returned item, we will process your refund or exchange within 14 business days.

Exchanges:

  • If you wish to exchange your footwear for a different size or model, follow the same return process mentioned above.
  • Availability of the desired exchange item will be confirmed upon receiving your return.

Please note that the above conditions are subject to change at our discretion. We recommend reviewing this page before making a purchase. If you have any questions or need assistance, feel free to contact our dedicated customer support team. Thank you for choosing VividStep for your footwear needs! Your satisfaction is our priority. Sincerely, The VividStep Team
